All About Me!

Hi! My name is Rishab Jain. I'm 14 years old and I live in the Bay Area with my parents, grandparents, and sister. Some of my hobbies are playing tennis, guitar, reading, and streaming Netflix. I love learning new things. I especially like learning about design, specifically architecture. I think architecture is fascinating because you can design something so imaginative but also very practical. For example, an architect can make a regular office building into something innovative and fun. That way, you can turn a practical space like an office complex into one that's more exciting and imaginative.
